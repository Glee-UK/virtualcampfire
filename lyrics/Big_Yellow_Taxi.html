    DDT is a pesticide that was responsible for a major decline in
    the populations of fish-eating birds, such as the bald eagle,
    brown pelican, peregrine falcon, and osprey.
    At the time Joni Mitchell wrote this song,
    there was a public outcry about its environmental impacts.
